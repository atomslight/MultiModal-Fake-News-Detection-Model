Neeraj Chopra has secured the Silver medal at Paris Olympics. Neeraj was seen as India's premier medal prospect from the start of the Paris Olympics 2024 games, and India's golden boy did not falter at the biggest stage. After qualifying for the final in just one attempt (89.34m), Neeraj Chopra rode on the momentum to finish at the podium.
