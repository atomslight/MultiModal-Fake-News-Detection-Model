After being crowned as the new men’s 100m Olympic Champion in Paris, which is one of the most dramatic sprints in the history of athletes, US’s Noah Lyles made headlines with his victory post on X(formally Twitter) by sharing his story that has the capability to inspire many. In his post, Noah shared that despite having Asthma, Allergies, Dyslexia, ADD, Anxiety, and Depression, he was able to win gold and create history.
