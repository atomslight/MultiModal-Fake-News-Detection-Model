Indian Ambassador to France, Jawed Ashraf, hosted Olympic bronze medallist #ManuBhaker and her coach Jaspal Rana at his Paris residence. During the visit, Ashraf gifted Bhaker an English translation of the Bhagavad Gita after she showed interest in the book, calling the gesture spontaneous.
