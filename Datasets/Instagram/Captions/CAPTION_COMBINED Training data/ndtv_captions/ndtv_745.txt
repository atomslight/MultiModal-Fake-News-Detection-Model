Unclaimed deposits with banks have witnessed a 26 per cent jump year on year to ₹ 78,213 crore at the end of March 2024, as per the RBI Annual Report released today.

The amount with the Depositor Education and Awareness Fund stood at ₹ 62,225 crore at the end of March 2023.
