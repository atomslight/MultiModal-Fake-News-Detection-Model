A 28-year-old person died due to his injuries after being hit by a speeding luxury car in Mumbai, police said.
The Worli Police said that the victim, Vinod Lad, died seven days after the accident. The police have registered a case against the driver, Kiran Indulkar and have arrested him. On July 20, Lad was hit by a speeding car on Abdul Gaffar Khan Road near Worli Sea Link. He died during his treatment on Saturday, July 27.
