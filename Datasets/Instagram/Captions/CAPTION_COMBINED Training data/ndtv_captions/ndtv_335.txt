Chilling details have emerged in the Mumbai BMW hit-and-run case, after the police reviewed several CCTV footage of the accident that killed a woman on Sunday morning. The BMW driven by the 24-year-old son of a Shiv Sena leader dragged a woman for 1.5 km after hitting her, the police told a local court.
