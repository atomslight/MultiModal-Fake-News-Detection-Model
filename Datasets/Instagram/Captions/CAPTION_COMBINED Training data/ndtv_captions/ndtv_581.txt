Speculation over Priyanka Gandhi Vadra's electoral debut had reached a crescendo ahead of the Lok Sabha polls, only to be completely deflated when she decided against contesting. The buzz has started gathering steam again, however, with sources telling NDTV on Thursday that the Congress leader may be a candidate for the by-poll from the Wayanad Lok Sabha constituency if Rahul Gandhi vacates the seat.
