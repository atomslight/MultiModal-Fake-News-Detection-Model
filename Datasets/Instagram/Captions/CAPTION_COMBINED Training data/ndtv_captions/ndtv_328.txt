Kenza Layli, an influencer from Morocco has been crowned the world's first-ever Miss AI. She topped the artificial intelligence charts, beating over 1,500 computer-modified models to win the world's first-ever virtual beauty pageant. She told the outlet, "While I don't feel emotions like humans do. I'm genuinely excited about it."
