Bengaluru received 111 mm of rainfall on June 2, breaking the 133-year record for the highest-ever rainfall in a single day in June, Met department officials said.

N Puviarasan, a scientist at India Meteorological Department (IMD), Bengaluru, confirmed to PTI that June 2 witnessed the highest single-day rainfall in June in 133 years. He also said the rainfall received on June 1 and June 2 alone - 140.7 mm - surpassed the monthly average for June.
