For many Indians, summer is synonymous with mangoes and mango-based dishes. This seasonal fruit, which has many varieties in the country, is among the most versatile and beloved.

Recently, India's mango delicacies received recognition on the global stage. Two of them were featured on Taste Atlas' list of the '10 Best Dishes with Mango', as per the guide's June 2024 rankings. One of the desi delights was even ranked no. 1 overall!
