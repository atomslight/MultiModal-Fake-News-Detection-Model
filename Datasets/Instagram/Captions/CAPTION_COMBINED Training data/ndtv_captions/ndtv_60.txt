A 29-year-old man was thrashed by a group of people allegedly for refusing to share hookah in a club here, police said. Five people have been arrested in connection with the case, they said.

As per the complaint filed by Pankaj Sharma, a resident of New Palam Vihar, the incident occurred on Saturday late night when he had gone to a club in a shopping mall in the section 109 area.
