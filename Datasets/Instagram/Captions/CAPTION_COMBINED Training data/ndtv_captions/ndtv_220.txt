Amid the buzz over a social media post by Uttar Pradesh's Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya, main Opposition Samajwadi Party's leader Akhilesh Yadav today said the people of Uttar Pradesh are fed up with infighting within the Yogi Adityanath-led BJP government.

"There is infighting in the government. BJP leaders are fighting among themselves. The people know about the corruption and are fed up with the game of thrones," Mr Yadav said at a party event.
