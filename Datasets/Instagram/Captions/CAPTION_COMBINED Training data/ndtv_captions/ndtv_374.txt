Retail prices of tomatoes in Delhi have surged to around ₹ 70-80 per kg on lower supply due to recent heatwave conditions.

Mother Dairy, a leading supplier of fruits and vegetables in Delhi-NCR, is selling tomatoes at ₹ 75 per kg through its retail 'Safal' stores. The local vendors are selling at ₹ 70-80 per kg.
