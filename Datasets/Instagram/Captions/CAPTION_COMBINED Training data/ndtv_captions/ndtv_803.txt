Anasuya Sengupta became the first Indian to win a top acting award at the Cannes Film Festival on Friday night. The little-known actress who has worked mainly as a production designer in Mumbai and lives in Goa, took home the Best Actress award at the Un Certain Regard segment of the festival. Anasuya Sengupta won the award for her gritty role in Bulgarian director Constantin Bojanov's film, The Shameless, which also features well-known actress Mita Vashist on its star cast.
