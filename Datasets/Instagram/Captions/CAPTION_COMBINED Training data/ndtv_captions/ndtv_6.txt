Mathias Boe, the coach of Chirag Shetty and Satwiksairaj Rankireddy, on Saturday announced his retirement from coaching following the ace Indian badminton pair’s exit from the ongoing Paris Olympics. Satwik and Chirag lost to Malaysia’s Aaron Chia and Soh Wooi Yik in the men’s doubles quarterfinals 21-13, 14-21, 16-21 in Paris on Thursday. Boe, a silver medal winner in the London Olympics, had joined Chirag and Satwik as their coach ahead of the Tokyo Olympics.
