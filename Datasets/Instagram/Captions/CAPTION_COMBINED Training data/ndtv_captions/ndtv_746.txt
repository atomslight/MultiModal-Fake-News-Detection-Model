As many as 22 people were killed and 57 were injured after a bus fell into a gorge in Jammu's Akhnoor on Thursday. The bus was carrying 50 passengers, most of them pilgrims from Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan and Haryana, who were headed to the famous Shiv Khori temple in Raesi district.
