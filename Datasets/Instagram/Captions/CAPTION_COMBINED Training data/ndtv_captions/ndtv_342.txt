#Opinion | "Men and women claiming to have direct access to god—of whichever kind—are nothing new. The claim of divinity is one of the oldest, most effective tricks to avert reproach and responsibility.

Commenting on his assumption as the new Superman more than a decade ago, actor Henry Cavill commented, 'India has a rich mythology and they're both (Superman and the heroes of Hindu mythology) rooted in the same thing - hope and goodness'. Unfortunately, the very hope and goodness of Indian spiritual traditions has been long exploited by charlatans pretending to be superheroes."
