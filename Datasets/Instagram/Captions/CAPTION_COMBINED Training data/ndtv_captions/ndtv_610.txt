A woman in Tripura allegedly strangled her nine-year-old son as she was frustrated with his "disobedient" behavior and couldn't handle him, as she told the police. The tragic case surfaced from Joynagar in Agartala on Monday evening. The woman, Suprabha Bal, confessed to the crime and was seen sitting near her son's body when the police arrived. She has been arrested and charged.
