A woman died after coming too close to a train while clicking a selfie. The incident took place in Mexico on Monday and the video of the incident is gaining traction on social media.

According to local outlets, some people had gathered to click a photo of a vintage train with steam engine near Hidalgo as it was passing by. The woman came too close to the tracks while positioning herself for a selfie with the approaching train, known as the 'Empress'.
