Popular sitcom 'Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah' fame Gurucharan Singh who played the role of 'Roshan Sodhi' is missing, claims his father.

He has lodged a complaint at Palam Police Station in Delhi.

The actor left for Mumbai on April 22 and had a flight from Delhi Airport, his father told the police adding he hadn't reached Mumbai.
