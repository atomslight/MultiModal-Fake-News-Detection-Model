Infosys founder Narayana Murthy's grandson, Ekagrah Rohan, will earn ₹ 4.2 crore in dividend income from the IT giant at just five months of age.

This comes after Mr Murthy gave the little boy 15 lakh shares (0.04% stake) of Infosys last month, valued at over ₹ 240 crore.
