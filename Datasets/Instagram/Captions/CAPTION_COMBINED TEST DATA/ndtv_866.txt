Two people were killed after a luxury Porsche car, driven by a minor, crashed into their motorcycle in Pune's Kalyani Nagar on Sunday, police said. The accident occurred at 3.15 am when Anish Awadiya and Ashwini Costa were returning home with friends on motorcycles after partying at a club, police said. Vedant Agarwal, 17, who was driving the Porsche Taycan, rammed the speeding vehicle into their bike at Kalyani Nagar junction.
