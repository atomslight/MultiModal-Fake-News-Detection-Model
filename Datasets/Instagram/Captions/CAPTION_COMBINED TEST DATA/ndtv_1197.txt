Doordarshan, the autonomous public broadcaster, has changed the colour of its logo from red to a distinct orange shade, sparking criticism from the opposition camp. DD News, the English news channel of Doordarshan, revealed the logo while sharing a new promotional video on X recently.

"While our values remain the same, we are now available in a new avatar. Get ready for a news journey like never before...Experience the all-new DD News," the caption read.
