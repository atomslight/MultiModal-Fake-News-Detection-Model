#ElectionsWithNDTV | A barber shop in Uttar Pradesh's Raebareli, where Congress leader Rahul Gandhi stopped for a haircut and beard trim, has seen a jump in the number of visitors after the celebrity visit.

Mithun Kumar, owner of the New Mumba Devi Hair Cutting Saloon in Raebareli's Lalganj, was in for a surprise on Monday when Mr Gandhi casually walked in and asked for a trim.
