The Enforcement Directorate (ED) has seized properties worth Rs. 97.79 Crore belonging to Raj Kundra in probe linked to a Bitcoin Ponzi scam. Raj Kundra is the husband of Bollywood actor Shilpa Shetty. The attached properties include a residential flat located in Juhu, currently registered in the name of actress Shilpa Shetty, a residential bungalow in Pune, and equity shares owned by Raj Kundra.
