A three-year-old girl suffocated to death in a locked car while her parents were attending a wedding function in Rajasthan’s Kota, police said today. According to the police, the incident occurred on Wednesday evening. They identified the girl as Gorvika Nagar. Pradeep Nagar, the girl’s father, had gone to attend a wedding function with his wife and two daughters. As the family reached the venue, the mother and elder daughter came out of the car.
