A 22-year-old Indian student, studying MTech in Australia, was allegedly stabbed to death when he tried to intervene in a fight between a group of Indian students, said the victim's uncle today. The incident took place yesterday at 9 pm local time in Melbourne. Another student was also seriously injured during the fight.

According to the victim's uncle, Navjeet Sandhu was "fatally stabbed in the chest" with a knife by another student when he was trying to mediate a confrontation in a rent related dispute between a group of Indian students.
