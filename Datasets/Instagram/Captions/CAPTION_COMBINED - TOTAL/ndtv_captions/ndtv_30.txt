US table tennis player Lily Ann Zhang regarded as the "Queen of American table tennis", reached the top 16 players in women's singles at the ongoing Paris Olympics. She is aged 28 and dreams about playing in front of her home crowd at the 2028 US Olympics. However, her parents, Linda Liu and Bob Zhang, immigrants from China, are not excited about her career and want her to have a "normal job."
