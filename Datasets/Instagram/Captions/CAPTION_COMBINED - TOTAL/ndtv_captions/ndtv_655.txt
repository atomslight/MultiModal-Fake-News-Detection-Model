In a rare move, the Tokyo Metropolitan Government is launching its own dating app to promote marriage and boost the plunging national birthrate. This new paid application, managed by a private entrepreneur, will be available this summer, Independent reported. Users will be required to submit documentation proving they are legally single and sign a letter stating they are willing to get married. The app will also require users to provide a tax certificate slip to prove their annual salary.
