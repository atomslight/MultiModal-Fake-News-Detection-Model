#Opinion | "We Indian women are all too acquainted with the belief system that there is something lacking in women who are childless. So much so that we could be forgiven for wondering what the caterwaul over Vance's comment is all about. But people like Vance in this country will have to get used to the idea that as women attain more education and financial independence, many more will choose not to have babies. Yes, it's fun to blame women for everything they do or not do, but we are fast reaching a stage where we don't give a damn."
