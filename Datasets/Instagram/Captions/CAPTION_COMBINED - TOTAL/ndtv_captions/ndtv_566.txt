Actress Saba Azad, who is known for Mujhse Fraaandship Karoge, Rocket Boys and Ladies Room, slammed “patriarchal mindset” for not being able to do the work she loves the most for two years - voice-overs. Recently, the actress took to the Stories section of her Instagram and shared a series of videos and pictures. The first picture is of her from a recording studio, on which she wrote: “Back in my natural habitat - recording a VO...after more than 2 yrs?”
