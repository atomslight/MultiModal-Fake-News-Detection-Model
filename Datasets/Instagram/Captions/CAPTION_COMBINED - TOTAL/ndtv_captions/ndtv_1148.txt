A wedding in Uttar Pradesh ended in a tragedy for a family after a conflict over the wedding gift resulted in a death. Chandra Prakash Mishra was beaten to death by his wife's family as she was upset with him for gifting a gold ring and a television to his sister on her wedding, the police said.

The incident was reported from Uttar Pradesh's Barabanki.
