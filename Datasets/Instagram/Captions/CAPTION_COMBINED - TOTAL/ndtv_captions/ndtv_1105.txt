Chennai Super Kings star cricketer MS Dhoni is well known for experimenting with his looks and his hairstyles over the years have been extremely popular among the fans. Ahead of CSK's crucial IPL 2024 encounter against Sunrisers Hyderabad on Sunday, Dhoni unveiled his new look - the 'samurai' style with a half ponytail.

The official social media handle of Chennai Super Kings shared the new look of Dhoni along with the caption - "7 Samurai".
