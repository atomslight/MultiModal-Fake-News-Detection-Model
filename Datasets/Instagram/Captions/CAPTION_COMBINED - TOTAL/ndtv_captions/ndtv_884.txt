AAP leader Swati Maliwal has filed a police complaint against Bibhav Kumar, an aide of Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal, alleging physical assault. Ms Maliwal in her complaint has alleged that the incident occurred in the drawing room of Mr Kejriwal's official residence, with the Chief Minister present in the house at the time.
