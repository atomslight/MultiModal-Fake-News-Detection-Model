Controversial social media influencer Bobby Kataria was arrested on Monday in connection with the alleged human trafficking of two Uttar Pradesh men, police said. The arrest came after the Gurugram Police and the National Investigation Agency (NIA) conducted a collaborative search operation at his Sector 109 residence.

The authorities seized a huge amount of cash and suspicious documents establishing his association with an international human trafficking network, according to the police.
