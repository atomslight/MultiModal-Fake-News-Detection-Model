Actor Chandrakanth, best known for working in daily soaps in Telugu, is no more. The actor allegedly died by suicide at his residence in Alkapur in Telangana on Friday. The news of his demise comes a few days after his co-star and close friend Pavithra Jayaram passed away in a car accident.

As per Chandrakanth's father's statement recorded by the police, the actor was depressed for the past couple of days.
