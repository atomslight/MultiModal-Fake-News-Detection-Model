Actor Sunil Lahiri, who played the role of Lakshman in Ramanand Sagar's 'Ramayan', said he was disappointed by the results of the Lok Sabha elections as the BJP failed to secure a majority. In a video message to his followers, the actor questioned whether the BJP's coalition government with its NDA partners could complete its full term without difficulties.
