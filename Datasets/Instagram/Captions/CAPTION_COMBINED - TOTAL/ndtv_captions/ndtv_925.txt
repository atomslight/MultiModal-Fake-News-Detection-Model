#DelhiNCR | In a horrific accident at a Noida housing complex last night, the brakes of a lift failed and it shot through the roof of the top floor. Three occupants of the lift were injured in the lift malfunction at the Paras Tierra Society in Sector 137. The incident occurring just months after an elderly woman died in a lift crash at the housing complex has triggered panic among its residents.
