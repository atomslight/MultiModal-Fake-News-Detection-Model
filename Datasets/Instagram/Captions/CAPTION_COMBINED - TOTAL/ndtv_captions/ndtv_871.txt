A beloved cat named Max has been awarded an honorary degree from Vermont State University's Castleton campus. Max, a tabby cat who lives near the university's main entrance, has become a fixture on campus, known for his friendly demeanour and love of attention. For several years, Max has been wandering the halls of the university, charming students and staff. The university recognised Max's contributions to the campus community with the honorary title of "Doctor of Litter-ature."
