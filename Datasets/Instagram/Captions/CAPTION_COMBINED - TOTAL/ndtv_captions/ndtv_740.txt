A government health university here has successfully performed a cataract surgery on a monkey, which had been admitted to the facility after getting burns due to an electric shock. According to Hisar's Lala Lajpat Rai University of Veterinary and Animal Sciences (LUVAS), this was the first ever surgery of cataract on a monkey in Haryana.
