A masked man choked a woman with a belt before dragging her behind a car and raping her on a New York street, a chilling CCTV video shows. The man, who has not been identified yet, had his face covered with a white cloth when he approached the woman from behind. In the video, he appears to have a brief conversation with her before she walks away. Seconds after she moves, the man pulls out a loop made of a belt, flings it over her head and violently yanks it dragging her to the ground.
