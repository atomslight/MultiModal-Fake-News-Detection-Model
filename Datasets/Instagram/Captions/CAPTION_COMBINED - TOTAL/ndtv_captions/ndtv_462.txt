Independent MP Pappu Yadav on Tuesday asked why Subodh Singh is still not arrested and who will talk about the youth of the country. He also wore a re-NEET t-shirt to his oath ceremony. Addressing the media, Pappu Yadav said "Why no re-examination for NEET? Why is Subodh Singh not being arrested? Who will talk about the youth? Nobody discussed NEET or special status for Bihar or the development of the state. So, I said so...I said Re-NEET..."
