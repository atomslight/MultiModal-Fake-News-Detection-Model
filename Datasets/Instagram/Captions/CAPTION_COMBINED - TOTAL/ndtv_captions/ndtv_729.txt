Draped in a traditional 'Mekhela Chador' and jewellery, Assam and northeast India's first artificially intelligent (AI) teacher 'Iris' promptly answered all the questions of students of a private school in Guwahati.

The humanoid listened to the question - what is haemoglobin? - and replied to the student with all the details, a teacher of the school said.
