The Food Safety Department of Telangana has been conducting inspections of many restaurants in various parts of Hyderabad in the past few days. On May 23, 2024, the officials visited the Madhapur area of the city. One of the places they raided was an outlet of the popular Bengaluru-based brand, The Rameshwaram Cafe.
