A 17-year-old youth preparing for the IIT-JEE competitive exam died by suicide in his room in Rajasthan's Kota city, police said on Sunday.

Ayush Jaiswal, a resident of Bihar's Motihari, was staying at a paying guest house near Samrat Chowk of Mahaveer Nagar area for two years to prepare for the competitive exam.
