A Bengaluru-based woman has been awarded a compensation of Rs 5 lakh by a consumer forum, nearly 20 years after a 3.2-centimetre surgical needle was left in her body following her surgery at a private hospital. Station House Officer (SHO) of Kotwali police station, Deendayal Pandey, said the two friends, Sameer (15) and Zakir Ahmed (16), are residents of Rajdepur.
