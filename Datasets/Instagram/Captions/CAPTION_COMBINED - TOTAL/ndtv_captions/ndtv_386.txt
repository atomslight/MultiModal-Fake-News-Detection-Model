#Opinion | "The fact remains that most Bollywood stars come to sets with their entire entourage, and the producer usually has no issues - sometimes, no choice - in coughing up money. It's the writers and other crew members who bear the brunt and get short-changed for their work.

Atika Chohan, known for Margarita With a Straw (2014), Chhapaak (2020) and Ulajh (2024), says, 'The production budgets leave no room for writers and development. No one takes risks with new ideas, while everyone bleeds money on actors who bring nothing new to the table but extreme losses. This is one of the biggest contributing factors to the current industry recession. In Bollywood, actors consider it an insult if they are not pampered like gods. It's embarrassing.'"
