Inspired by the Iron Man superhero character, a man in China used magnets to remove metal shards embedded in his thigh but ended up in a hospital. The daring attempt of the man, surnamed Jiang, amused many social media users in China.

The said scene appeared in the first Iron Man movie in which Tony Stark, who later turns into a superhero, is attacked while travelling in a military convoy. Some metal shards are embedded in his chest, which he removes by using an "electromagnet".
