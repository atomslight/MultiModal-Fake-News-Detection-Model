Ram Chet, a cobbler in Uttar Pradesh's Sultanpur, is no longer just another man stitching torn sandals and polishing leather shoes on the roadside. He is now the man in demand, people are coming to his makeshift shop to click selfies, officials are asking him if all is well and passers-by are stopping their cars to greet him.

All thanks to a visit by Congress leader Rahul Gandhi.
