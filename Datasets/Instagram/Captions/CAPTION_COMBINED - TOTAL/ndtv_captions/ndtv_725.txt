AK-47 and M-16 rifles from Pakistan, minor sharpshooters and an escape plan to Sri Lanka - the police have uncovered chilling details of an assassination plot to kill Bollywood actor Salman Khan.

The Lawrence Bishnoi gang had allegedly conspired to strike Mr Khan's car in Maharashtra's Panvel, armed with weapons sourced from a Pakistani arms supplier.
